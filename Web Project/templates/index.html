Trending
Released in 1990, Enjoy the Silence is Depeche Mode’s most iconic track, blending dark synth-pop with haunting lyrics. The song became an international hit and remains one of the band’s signature anthems. Released in 2023, "Ghosts Again" is Depeche Mode's first single from their album Memento Mori. Co-written with Richard Butler of The Psychedelic Furs, the track blends reflective lyrics with a synth-pop melody, exploring themes of mortality and the afterlife. The song's introspective tone and emotional depth have resonated with audiences worldwide.
